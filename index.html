Advance Building System EMMEDUE

The EMMEDUE panel comprises of a layer of mesh on either side of EPS core welded together by steel ortogonal (trusses) which penetrates through EPS core. The panels are joined together in a desired configuration on site and sprayed on both sides with shotcrete to form a sandwich type construction.

Fire Resistance Self-extinguishing panels, reinforced concrete as external coat to sides of the panel - inhibits combustion
Seismic Performance Lightweight, low seismic mass, rigid with two sheets of reinforced plaster
Wind Performance Laboratory tested for cyclonic winds
Water Penetration Exterior coating of 25mm thickness is applied after shotcreting for water proofing
Thermal Behaviour Thickness and density of panel can be customised for desired effects. A 150mm wall provides thermal insulation similar to a 400 mm solid masonry wall
Acoustic Behaviour Panel can be coupled with sound absorbing material
Height of building Low rise to medium rise
Installation/ Assembly Lightweight (3.5 to 5kg/m2). A single worker can handle a 3m2 wall. Very few heavy equipment needed
MEP Design Slots can be made wherever and wheneverrequired for the linings of MEP
Maintenance EPS Core panels factory made. Maintenance guide needs to be provided by the contractor
Green Building Standard Polystyrene provided eliminates thermal bridges and ducts within the panel
Finishes flexibility Variety of finishes are possible
Building elements Load bearing panels, Partition panels, floor, staircase, shear wall, any geometric shaped panels
Ease of fabrication Offsite - factory made panels
Local resources Labour requirement is considerably less. Skilled labour is majorly needed
Type of building A mass housing project will increase the requirement of the panel system and thus viable
International Projects Morocco, Algeria, South Africa, Kenya, Austria, Malaysia, Ireland, Romania, Australia; developed about 30 years back
Indian Projects Model single story housing unit in bangalore with panels imported from Italy
Certificate Holder M/s EMMEDUE S.P.A (Italy), M/s BK Chemtech Engineering Pvt. Ltd. (Bangalore)
Limitations Clean and closed storage to prevent exposure to sunlight (< 1 month exposure at max)

Rapid Panels

The Rapid Panel is a prefabricated assembly of high-strength steel wire forming a panel with a core of expanded polystyrene (EPS). During construction, Rapid Panels are installed as walls and/or slabs. Specified mixtures of mortar or concrete are applied to the surfaces of the panels to complete the structure. The basic unit of the Rapid Panel is the zig-zag truss.

Fire Resistance 1 hr: Panels covered with 29 mm thick cement plaster, 2 hr: Panels covered with 25mm cement + 12 mm light weight gypsum, Flammable material should not be stacked with it
Seismic Performance Acts as a continuous shear wall system. Can be used for structures upto G+5 in Zone 5 having poor soil conditions to provide an economical and robust structure
Wind Performance Laboratory tested
Water Penetration Exterior coating needs to be provided
Thermal Behaviour Transmittance U = 0.503 W/mK
Acoustic Behaviour An extra coating is needed to reduce the sound
Height of building Low rise to medium rise
Installation/ Assembly Self-connection system - mesh on the end of the panel shall be used to join the panels
MEP Design Removing polystyrene from the position of installation
Maintenance No special maintenance is required during intended life
Green Building Standard Polystyrene provided eliminates thermal bridges and ducts within the panel
Finishes flexibility Mortar or concrete finish
Building elements Wall panels, roof, floor
Ease of fabrication Offsite - Fully automated environment. Panels are manufactured in fixed sizes - need to cut according to requirement
Local resources Does not require shuttering/formwork as EPS filler acts in this capacity. Skilled manpower needed
Type of building Small housing projects have been tested
International Projects Developed in USA. Panels are manufactured in Mexico
Indian Projects No manufacturing plant in India at present; IIT Madras has certified the Rapid panel system for live loads defined in IS 875 (part 2): 1987; GF, G+2 - Bangalore - Each 200 sqm; CSI church - Bangalore - 350 sqm; S+2 - Bangalore - 120 sqm; G+3 - Bhubaneshwar - 200 sqm; S + 2 + H - Bangalore - 180 sqm; B+G - Coorg - 300 sqm
Certificate Holder M/s Worldhaus Construction Pvt. Ltd. (Bangalore)
Limitations Maximum length of panel = 5m; Panels are 1.22m wide with heights varying from 1.52m to 3.55m with minimum thickness of 100mm or more; Panels need to be cut as they come in fixed sizes.; Minimum grade of concrete is M20

Quickbuild 3D Panels

QuickBuild Panel system consists of a welded wire space frame integrated with a polystyrene insulation core. The wall panel is placed in position and a wythe of concrete is applied to both sides. The wall panel receives its strength and rigidity from the diagonal cross wires welded to the welded-wire fabric on each side. This combination produces a truss behavior, which provides rigidity and shear terms for a full composite behavior.

Fire Resistance Polystyrene is self-extinguishing and the reinforced concrete layer inhibits combustion. Shall withstand for 2 hours
Seismic Performance Lightweight panels - thus low seismic mass. However are rigid due to two sheets of reinforced plaster
Wind Performance Monolithic structure of the panel in conjunction with concrete withstands high winds
Water Penetration Render to be applied outside; DPC to be installed at ground level to prevent rising damp; Sealants around windows and doors; Panels are excellent in preventing condensation/ absorption on interior walls
Thermal Behaviour Rigid foam insulation provides long term thermal resistance that does not need to be adjusted for age
Acoustic Behaviour Excellent dampening capability. Further enhanced by increasing the core thickness. Upto 50 dB insulation
Height of building Low rise to medium rise
Installation/ Assembly No major heavy equipment needed to install
MEP Design Wire mesh is cut and the EPS layer blowed to accommodate the services before plastering
Maintenance Exterior coating may be applied to provide additional protection to the reinforcement
Green Building Standard Thermally advantageous. Can be manufactured in varying thicknesses to meet the environmental design criteria to deliver the range of R-value
Finishes flexibility Fine plastering can be done
Building elements Exterior and interior load bearing and non-load bearing walls, floors, stair case
Ease of fabrication Offsite - factory made panels
Local resources Trained and skilled labour is required
Type of building Small housing projects have been tested
International Projects No international projects
Indian Projects Axial compression test and static flexural test done by IIT Madras; Christ College - TN; Meridian Hotel - Bangalore; Bethany school - Bangalore; Sure Energy systems Pvt. Ltd. - AP; VTRC Ponmeni - TN; SERC Taramani - Chennai; VME Reality - TN; KPCL Wood house - TN; Champs empowering education - AP
Certificate Holder M/s Beardsell Ltd. (Chennai)
Limitations Do not overload partition walls on one side. Spray concrete on both sides alternatively. Panels should not be directly laid on the ground and not exposed to sunlight before installation for more than a month

Concrewall Panel System

The Concrewall System is an industrial system for the construction of structural walls of reinforced concrete for building in single panel up to G+3. The system is composed of a factory produced panel of undulated (wave shape) polystyrene covered on both sides by an electro-welded zinc coated square mesh of galvanized steel and linked by 40 connectors per sq m made of high-elastic-limit 3mm dia wires realizing a 3 dimensional hyper-static reinforced steel.

Fire Resistance Self-extinguishing type EPS
Seismic Performance Lightweight implies lesser earthquake forces. Design can made to make it safe
Wind Performance Design of roof to wall connections to be specific to ensure that roof is restrained against uplift
Water Penetration No dripping or leakage through slab during 24 hr ponding; Inner face of the wall was observed to be free from permissible limits of dampening
Thermal Behaviour Reduction in outside and inside temperature was recorded upto 5.8 degrees
Acoustic Behaviour Reduction of sound level by 35 dB
Height of building Low rise to medium rise (up to G+3)
Installation/ Assembly Trained workers and special equipments needed for installation
MEP Design Wire mesh is cut and the EPS layer blowed to accomodate the services before plastering
Maintenance A separate manual needs to be given by the contractor on how to carry out the maintenance
Green Building Standard Thermal insulated panels help in increasing thermal comfort (Thermal transmittance Up = 0.557 W/m2K)
Finishes flexibility Concrete is the main finish. Can add above that
Building elements Load bearing and non-load bearing walls, shear walls, roof, floor slab, infill walls
Ease of fabrication Panels are assembled in-situ and shotcreted
Local resources Trained workers are needed to handle the erection, clamping, infilling reinforcement and concrete
Type of building Economical for mass housing only
International Projects Italy, Spain, Hungary, Russia, Saudi Arabia, Oman, Libya, Mexico, France, Venezuela, Brazil and Argentina (G+1 to G+6)
Indian Projects Plants in India: West - Pune, East - Odisha, North - Punjab; Buildings: G+3 - Odisha - Mass Housing, Hostel and Hospital buildings - Punjab, Anganwadi buildings - across india, Used as infill walls in different regions
Certificate Holder M/s Schnell Home (Italia)
Limitations Single floors or roofs upto 5m x 5m. Panels are 1.23 m wide. Should not be laid directly on the ground. Sunlight exposure before installation should be less than one month

Light Gauge Steel Structural System (LGSS)

Light Gauge Framed Steel Structure (LGFSS) is based on factory made galvanized light gauge steel components produced by the cold forming method assembled as panels at site forming structural steel framework of a building and varying wall and floor construction as detailed in this Certificate. The panels are assembled on site with screws and bolts to form the internal and separating walls and inner leaf of the external walls of a building and floors & ceiling.

Fire Resistance Wall panels to be used need to be fire resistant
Seismic Performance Lightweight leads to significant reduction in earthquake forces. Highly ductile.
Wind Performance The cladding sheets used need to be designed to resist wind
Water Penetration The wall panels used need to be coated with water proofing material if not already
Thermal Behaviour External layer of insulation material is installed in conjunction with the panels
Acoustic Behaviour External layer of sound proofing is installed in conjunction with the panels
Height of building Low rise to medium rise (upto G+3)
Installation/ Assembly Basic building elements are cold formed which can be prefabricated on site and connected using screws and bolts
MEP Design Pre-planning is required to punch holes at the necessary places. Can incorporate all types of services
Maintenance Can be used for temporary structures too. No special maintenance is required during intended work life
Green Building Standard MThe cold form steel can be reused.
Finishes flexibility Cement particle board or gypsum board.
Building elements Floor, roof, walls, All types of architectural features like coving, boxes, cantilevers, projections, infill walls, mezzanine floors
Ease of fabrication Fully integrated computerised system - accuracy upto 1 mm
Local resources Trained and skilled workforce is required
Type of building Economical for small projects too
International Projects Residential projects in North America, Australia, Japan
Indian Projects Design details of G+3 two bed room flat considering wind load, seismic load etc. complete duly vetted by IIT Madras; 12 blocks of Staff Quarters at Prasadha, Raigarh and Construction of 2nd floor extension of Girls hostel of GP Jindal Institute of Technology, Raigarh.
Certificate Holder M/s JB Fabinfra Pvt. Ltd (New Delhi)
Limitations G+3 without any composition change. For greater heights, hot rolled structures composition needs to be used. Not suitable for buildings with vibrations (machine workshop)
